# Installation Guide

1. Download the `ultimate_bridge` folder.
2. Place it in your server's `resources` directory.
3. Add `ensure ultimate_bridge` to your `server.cfg`.
4. (Optional) Configure `config.lua` to your preference, though it will auto-detect most systems.

## Prerequisites
- `oxmysql` or `mysql-async` or `ghmattimysql`
- One of the supported frameworks (ESX, QBCore, or Qbox) or Standalone.
